Robo FootBall 2K24

Aditya Degree College, Kakinada.

Welcome to Robo FootBall 2K24 event conducted by john Mc charty club(AI) from aditya degree college, kakinada.




Registrations Details:

  • The Robo Football 2K24 event is conducted with the intention of bringing all AI branch students together in one place.
  • This event is conducted by the Joch McCharathy Club of AI at Aditya Degree College, Kakinada.
  • The event will be conducted during the first week of October.
  • In this event, we are conducting a football game between robot cars.
  • Only AI students from each college can participate in this competition.
  • Each college can participate with only five teams in this competition.
  • Every robot car is equipped with only a gyro sensor for handling.
  • The registration fee will be announced shortly.
  • The winners are classified into two categories: first prize with a cashprize of 25,000/- and second prize with a cashprize of 15,000/-.
  • Participating teams can be classified into two types:
    1. Single's Team
    2. Double's Team


    • a. Single's Team

  • In singles, each team consists of only three members.
  • The car can perform both defender and attacker functions.
  • The rules and regulations can be provided within a short span of time.
  • If you want to register as a singles team, click the register button.


    • b. Double's Team

  • In Double's, each team consists of only six members.
  • In double's, team can build two cars: one for defense and another for attack.
  • The rules and regulations can be provided within a short span of time.
  • If you want to register as a Double's Team, click the register button.




    Note:-
