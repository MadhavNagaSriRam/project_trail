About College

Aditya Degree College has consistently demonstrated its excellence. A large number of students have secured placements in top companies, reflecting the college's effective curriculum, dedicated faculty, and strong industry partnerships.

About Gruop

In 2020, our journey into the world of Artificial Intelligence began under the esteemed guidance of our Dean, Siddarth Reddy Sir. Our AI course has been skillfully led by Kaushik Adiraju, whose expertise and passion have greatly enriched our learning experience.This group has its own club, "John McCarthy," named in honor of the AI pioneer, which serves as a hub for innovation and collaboration among students.

About Club

The club, named "John McCarthy," was proudly established by the first batch of AIR students, symbolizing their dedication to the field of Artificial Intelligence. Every year, the Sciland program is held, bringing together the brightest minds from various disciplines. However, it is the AI students who consistently dominate, securing the top position year after year, a testament to their innovation and excellence.
